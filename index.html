Digital India 2047 envisions a technologically advanced and inclusive nation.
It builds upon the success of the Digital India initiative launched in 2015.

The Vision of Digital India 2047



An India where innovation breathes, possibilities ignite, and every citizen thrives in a tapestry woven with technology and human ingenuity.
By 2047, as India celebrates its centenary of independence, we envision a nation transformed – a Viksit Bharat where digital power isn't just a tool, but the very heartbeat of progress.
This isn't just a plan; it's a promise, built on three foundational pillars that will elevate every life:

1. The Digital Lifeline: Infrastructure for All ~
A robust, secure, and ubiquitous digital foundation. This pillar ensures every citizen has universal access to high-speed internet, a digital identity (Aadhaar), and financial inclusion through digital payments, making essential services a guaranteed utility, not a privilege.

2. Seamless Governance: Services at Your Fingertips ~
Governance that is responsive, transparent, and immediate. By digitizing all government services and records, this pillar ensures 'services on demand' through a single window. It eliminates paper, reduces corruption, and uses smart data to deliver justice, healthcare, and education efficiently.

3. Empowered Citizens: Igniting Every Indian's Potential ~
Cultivating a nation of digitally literate innovators. This focuses on achieving universal digital literacy, making information accessible in all Indian languages, and fostering a startup ecosystem powered by AI, IoT, and Quantum computing to solve local challenges and compete globally.
